About SasksVoice

SasksVoice is a community-powered content platform that gives everyone a voice. Submit your images and captions, and once reviewed, they get published directly to our Instagram page.

Our Mission

We believe everyone has something worth sharing. SasksVoice bridges the gap between content creators and audiences by providing a simple, accessible platform for community expression.

How Submissions Work

  • Free Posts: Standard submissions that go through our review queue.
  • Promotional Posts: $1–$2 for priority review and guaranteed publishing.

Content Guidelines

All submissions go through our automated moderation system to ensure community safety. Posts containing hate speech, explicit content, spam, or harmful material will be automatically flagged and rejected.
